ðŸ”µ Pull Workout 2

Primary

  • Latissimus Dorsi (Lats): Pulling power
  • Trapezius (Traps): Scapular movement, high pulls
  • Biceps Brachii: Elbow flexion in all pulls

Secondary

  • Rhomboids: Scapular retraction
  • Rear Deltoids: Active in face/high pulls

  • Triceps Brachii: In triceps extension portion

Stabilizer

  • Rotator Cuff: Shoulder joint support
  • Core: Stabilization in deadlifts and rows

Primary

  • Deltoids (Front & Side): Pressing and lateral raises
  • Triceps Brachii: Pressing and extensions

Secondary

  • Biceps Brachii: Curls in supersets
  • Rotator Cuff: External rotation work
  • Serratus Anterior: Push-up variations

ðŸ”´ Push Workout 2

Primary

  • Deltoids (Front): Overhead pressing
  • Pectoralis Major (Chest): Pressing and adduction
  • Triceps Brachii: Pressing and close-grip work

Secondary

  • Biceps Brachii: Curls in supersets
  • Upper Back (Traps, Rhomboids): Abduction rows
  • Rear Deltoids: In abduction rows
  • Serratus Anterior: Pushup Plus

Stabilizer

  • Rotator Cuff: Joint integrity and shoulder health
  • Core: Trunk stability in overhead and pushups
